Casing

  • The thing that encases the fan and the rest of the components.
  • Made of plain white ABS plastic, shaped like an upside-down snowman.
  • Manufacturers claim it has an ergonomic grip (it sort of does).
  • Quite durable and cheap to manufacture, but is not easy to disassemble.
  • Measures 13.5 x 8.2 x 2.3cm.

Li-Po battery

  • Small light rechargeable battery with 3.7v
  • Adhesive layer that sticks to the PCB to keep it in place
  • Provides power to fan and LED
  • Easy to implement, cheap to obtain, and lightweight
  • Fire hazard if punctured
  • 3.5 x 3 cm

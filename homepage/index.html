Overview

Fan

This is a simple, portable handheld fan. The casing made it quite hard to disassemble, and in the process, we did accidentally break some stuff, and lose a few parts in the process, but that doesn't matter.

GPU

Our second product - an old GTX 1650. It generates graphics for computers, but the chip overheated, so I disassembled it. A classmate ended up rolling the thermal pads into a ball, so we couldn't fully return it to its original state. It was quite easy to disassemble, as everything was held together by screws, aside from the fan.

Lamp

Our final product (the lamp), although it had screws to unscrew some things, did require a certain level of strength to take apart the casing. It was fine.

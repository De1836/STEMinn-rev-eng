Case

This is the PCB that the lights are soldered onto. It recieves power from the battery, and distributes it to the lights. It has wires soldered onto it from the first PCB to get the on/off signal from the push button. It is mounted onto the bottom of the case and is made of aluminium, and most likely copper for the traces. However, the PCB, due to being made of aluminum flexes and bends, which may cause some issues. There seem to be no issues for that, so it may not be a huge issue.
